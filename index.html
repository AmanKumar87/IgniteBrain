Frequently Asked Questions

01

Who are the courses for?

Our courses are designed for students aged 8-18, from absolute beginners with no prior experience to intermediate learners looking to advance their skills.

02

What do I need to get started?

Just a computer with an internet connection! All our software is browser-based, so there are no complicated installations. For robotics courses, you'll need one of our Creator's Toolkits.

03

Can I cancel my subscription?

Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time, no questions asked. You'll retain access to your courses until the end of the current billing period.
