What is the Painting Olympiad?

The Ignite Brain Painting Olympiad is a celebration of art and creativity. It provides a prestigious platform for school students across India to express their unique perspectives on inspiring themes. We believe that art is a powerful form of communication and a vital part of a well-rounded education.

This competition is designed to encourage artistic exploration, develop fine motor skills, and give young artists the recognition they deserve for their hard work and talent.

Themes & Guidelines

Themes are designed to be imaginative and age-appropriate.

Primary Level (Classes 1-4)

Theme for 2025: "My Favourite Animal Friend"

  • Permitted Mediums: Crayons, Oil Pastels, Color Pencils.
  • Guideline: Submissions must be on A4 size paper.
  • Focus: Bright colors, clear depiction, and imagination.

Middle Level (Classes 5-8)

Theme for 2025: "A Dream of a Greener Earth"

  • Permitted Mediums: Watercolors, Poster Colors, Acrylics.
  • Guideline: Submissions must be on A3 size paper.
  • Focus: Composition, color theory, and message clarity.

Senior Level (Classes 9-12)

Theme for 2025: "Harmony in Chaos"

  • Permitted Mediums: Any medium, including oil, charcoal, and mixed media.
  • Guideline: Minimum size A3. No maximum size limit.
  • Focus: Originality, technical skill, and depth of concept.
